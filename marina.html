Welcome, Friends

Two-Rivers Resort lies at the confluence of the Spokane and Columbia river and is the secluded getaway you’ve been waiting for. Fun, relaxing, and solitude are words you won’t forget when visiting.

We’re located just 25 miles north of the beautiful town of Davenport, Washington. Whether it’s camping, fishing or boating Two Rivers meets the needs of every family big or small.



Preserve The History

Immerse yourself in the history of the area and it's people. Be surrounded by the culture and revel in the beauty. Breathe in the nature!

Indulge in the Outdoors

Camp out in one of the cabins, or bring your own camping gear. Two Rivers gives you options, no matter the plan.

Land and Water: Explore It All

Traverse the trails, swim and sail, party with Houseboat Adventures, or kick back from the comfort of your RV. Explore the rivers and cliffs.

    Spokane Tribal Enterprises


    Two Rivers Resort has been proudly powered by the direction of Spokane Tribal Enterprises.

    STE has been striving to expand and promote business growth within the Spokane Tribe.

    The Mission of the Spokane Tribal Enterprises is to promote, develop and enhance sustainable economic and community service for for-profit business opportunities. STE leverages those opportunities to create employment, development and training opportunities for tribal members and Enterprise staff.
