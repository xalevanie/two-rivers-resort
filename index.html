Welcome, Friends

Two-Rivers Resort lies at the confluence of the Spokane and Columbia river and is the secluded getaway you’ve been waiting for. Fun, relaxing, and solitude are words you won’t forget when visiting.

We’re located just 25 miles north of the beautiful town of Davenport, Washington. Whether it’s camping, fishing or boating Two Rivers meets the needs of every family big or small.

Preserve This History

Immerse yourself in the history of the area and it's people. Be surrounded by the culture and revel in the beauty.

Indulge in the Outdoors

Camp out in one of the cabins, or bring your own camping gear. Two Rivers gives you options, no matter the plan. Sleep in your own bed among nature.

Land and Water: Explore It All

Traverse the trails, swim and play, party with Houseboat Adventures, or kick back from the comfort of your RV. Explore the rivers and cliffs.

It's time to get outside and get excited!

From camping to Houseboat fun, Two Rivers has everything you'll need for a summer adventure.
